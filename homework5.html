Shandong cuisine

Shandong Province is located in the lower reaches of the Yellow River, with a mild climate. There are rivers, lakes, hills, plains, seas and other diverse landforms in Shandong Province, which makes the selection of food materials of Shandong cuisine extremely rich and balanced: the frequency of each category of Shandong cuisine is between 15% and 18%.
